## 2. Best Commercial Websites — Design + Usability (Researched 2026)

Curated list of the world's best-designed commercial sites. Organised by category, ranked within each.

### SaaS / B2B (the gold standard)
- [Linear](https://linear.app) — live agent demo embedded in a real-looking issue ticket; segmented CTAs without clutter
- [Stripe](https://stripe.com) — iconic gradient hero, infinite logo carousel, trust via volume metrics
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com) — bento-grid product taxonomy, system-aware theme toggle
- [Attio](https://attio.com) — monochromatic CRM with light-to-dark scroll transitions
- [Cal.com](https://cal.com) — 3-step workflow visual, frictionless sign-up
- [Plain](https://plain.com) — crisp typography, calm motion, one of the cleanest support tooling pricing pages
- [Resend](https://resend.com) — developer-first IA, code-in-context demos
- [Clerk](https://clerk.com) — auth flows shown as actual interactive components — "show, don't tell"
- [Cluely](https://cluely.com) — animated desktop mockup, before/after demo, gradient hero
- [Supabase](https://supabase.com) — editorial dark theme, scroll-pinned product chapters

### Healthtech / medtech / clinical tools
- [Heidi Health](https://www.heidihealth.com) — specialty-segmented landing pages, compliance badges in nav
- [Everlab](https://www.everlab.com.au) — rotating biomarker marquee, 100+ doctor gallery as authority signal
- [Lyra Technologies](https://lyratechnologies.com.au) — scroll-driven proof density (YC/a16z badges, portfolio grid). The reference for scroll-storytelling
- [Nutromics](https://www.nutromics.com) — cinematic biotech motion; molecular animations tied to product narrative
- [Diag-Nose](https://www.diag-nose.io) — editorial medtech feel; clinical branding that doesn't read sterile
- [OpenEvidence](https://www.openevidence.com) — clinical-search UI shown directly on the landing page
- [Tempus](https://www.tempus.com) — data-dense medtech with Sankey-like viz and live trial counters
- [Neuralink](https://neuralink.com) — long-form scroll narrative for a regulated product

### Marketplace / two-sided platforms (most relevant to StatDoctor)
- [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.com) — canonical two-sided trust pattern
- [Faire](https://www.faire.com) — explicit dual onboarding ("Sign up to buy" / "Sign up to sell"). **Closest analogue to StatDoctor**
- [DoorDash](https://www.doordash.com) — three-audience IA without splintering brand
- [Turo](https://turo.com) — two CTAs above the fold with separate motion treatments
- [Upwork](https://www.upwork.com) — best-in-class supply-side dashboard previews

### Editorial / motion-driven (Awwwards-tier, still usable)
- [Lando](https://lando.com) — Awwwards Site of the Year 2025
- [Scout Motors](https://scoutmotors.com) — Awwwards Developer SOTY 2025
- [Igloo Inc](https://igloo.inc) — editorial, motion-rich, still routes cleanly to product CTAs
- [Basement Studio](https://basement.studio) — reference for scroll-pinned chapters
- [Oxide Computer](https://oxide.computer) — long-form editorial product page; technical storytelling without losing buyers

### Consumer products with great commercial pages
- [Apple — iPhone](https://www.apple.com/iphone) — scroll-pinned chapter nav, modal feature reveals. **The reference for sequenced narrative**
- [Arc](https://arc.net) — testimonial-as-hero, progressive feature reveal, dual-OS download in one tight viewport
- [Raycast](https://www.raycast.com) — interactive QWERTY, glass morphism, Pro upsell after value
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com) — 8-slide capability carousel; template for multi-step product loops
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) — persona-segmented homepages without fragmenting brand
- [Linear — Method](https://linear.app/method) — long-form editorial inside a SaaS site

## 3. Locum Doctor Competitors — Best-Designed (Researched Globally)

The category is design-poor. These are the only 8 worth studying, ranked best to worst.

1. [Patchwork Health](https://patchwork.health) (UK) — strongest brand voice, empathetic editorial copy, considered typography
2. [Nomad Health](https://nomadhealth.com) (US) — closest US analog to "Uber for clinicians"; scroll-driven milestone timeline
3. [CareRev](https://www.carerev.com) (US) — coral/pink accent breaks healthcare's blue-everything default; metric-forward
4. [ShiftKey](https://www.shiftkey.com) (US) — best photographic direction; full-bleed hero of real clinicians
5. [Locumate](https://locumate.ai) (AU) — best interactive element in the category: live ROI calculator in hero
6. [Florence](https://florenceapp.com) (UK) — hand-drawn SVG illustration system that humanises healthcare
7. [DirectShifts](https://www.directshifts.com) (US) — cleanest of the modern US locum players
8. [Cherry Health](https://cherry.health) (Canada) — only Canadian locum site attempting a modern landing page
