## What StatDoctor is
StatDoctor is Australia's first locum doctor marketplace. We connect doctors
directly with hospitals, no agencies, no hidden fees, zero commission, ever.
Doctors keep 100% of their posted rate. Hospitals pay a flat platform fee
instead of agency markups. Built by doctors, for doctors.

The company is Australian (ABN 63 671 408 692), based at
2/1 Louise St, Underwood QLD 4119.

## Who runs it

Founder story (short version): Anu worked public ED for years and picked up
regional locum shifts on weekends. Every booking went through an agency with
late-night calls, shifting rates, and redundant paperwork. Seven months after a
one-off locum shift, the hospital asked her to stay on, and the agency demanded
a 25% buy-out fee (three months of pay) for an introduction made half a year
earlier. The hospital paid the fee to make it stop. That was the moment she
decided to build StatDoctor.

In January 2025 the company launched in Victoria with a handful of rural locum
doctors. Portland District Health was the first hospital to post a shift. A
doctor accepted that same evening. Today the team partners with 47 hospitals
across Australia and has saved them over $200,000 in agency fees.

## For DOCTORS, how it works

Four steps, in the app:

1. Browse: Real-time feed of every shift in your state. Filter by specialty,
   rate, location, and date.
2. Rate: The rate is posted by the hospital, visible upfront. No agency
   markup. No surprise on payday.
3. Apply: Two taps. Credentials prefilled from your profile. Most hospitals
   confirm inside the hour.
4. Paid: Money in your account within 48 hours of completing the shift.
   No invoicing.

## For HOSPITALS, how it works

Four steps:

1. Post: Post a shift in 90 seconds. Specialty, date, hours, rate, location.
   Live to 400+ verified doctors immediately.
2. Review: See applicants with credentials side-by-side: AHPRA registration,
   indemnity, CV, ratings, history.
3. Confirm: One tap to confirm. Doctor notified instantly. Most shifts
   confirmed inside 30 minutes.
4. Manage: Track active, confirmed, and archived shifts in one dashboard.
   Settle in 48 hours. Doctor keeps 100% of the posted rate.

Median time-to-fill is under 24 hours. Same-day urgent shifts often fill
in 30 minutes.

## Pricing, for hospitals

Flat annual pricing. No per-shift fees. No buy-out fees. No per-seat charges.

- Single Hospital plan: Starting from $10,000/year.
  Includes unlimited doctors and unlimited shifts, locum AND permanent hiring,
  and locum-to-permanent conversion at $0 extra. AHPRA-verified doctors.

- Health Service / State Service plan: Starting from $50,000/year.
  Manage up to 10 hospitals through a single admin account. Everything in
  the single-hospital plan, applied across the network.

Comparison with traditional agencies:
- Agency fee: 15-25%       →  StatDoctor fee: 0% from doctor
- Time to fill: 2-10 days  →  StatDoctor: 2-48 hours
- Credential checks: manual, ongoing   →  StatDoctor: continuous & automated
- Quality control: agency-mediated     →  StatDoctor: direct ratings & reviews
- Payment: via agency      →  StatDoctor: direct, 48 hours

If a hospital wants to hire a locum permanently, that costs $0 extra.
Agencies typically charge 15-25% of first-year salary as a buy-out fee.
We charge nothing, that arrangement is between the hospital and the doctor.

## Pricing, for doctors

Free. The rate the hospital posts is the rate the doctor receives. Hospitals
pay the platform fee, never the doctor.

## Verification

Every doctor uploads: AHPRA registration, current indemnity certificate, and an
up-to-date CV. We verify on signup and refresh checks continuously. Some shifts
may require additional items (e.g. recent CPR certificate), these are shown
before the doctor applies. Hospitals see verification status on every applicant.

## Payment

- Doctors are paid within 48 hours of completing a shift, direct to their
  bank account. No invoicing, no agency, no chasing.
- Hospitals pay through the platform. The platform fee is annual flat,
  not per-shift.

## Cancellations

If a hospital cancels within 24 hours of a shift, our cancellation policy
guarantees a partial payout to the doctor. Full terms are visible in the
app before any shift is accepted.

## Travel & accommodation (for rural/regional shifts)

Most rural and regional hospitals reimburse travel and cover accommodation.
The listing shows exactly what's included before the doctor applies. No
chasing the hospital after the fact.
